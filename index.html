Tāpiri Organic Café:

A Wholesome Experience


More than just a café. It is a community hub that celebrates the Māori culture and values of manaakitanga (hospitality), kaitiakitanga (guardianship) and whanaungatanga (kinship).

The café's name means "to add" or "to join" in te reo Māori, reflecting its vision of bringing people together through food and friendship.
